About Me

My name is Justus Brown, a current undergraduate at the State University at Brockport. Currently working towards my Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, you'll find that i truely enjoy working with computers. Along with my love of computers, i enjoy spending my time with friends, familly, and my dog Shadow. I'm a huge fan of games; board games, card games, and video games. Currently enjoy spending my time playing card games like Magic: The Gathering and Hearthstone. When i'm playing Magic, i'll more times than not by playing some sort of control style deck with Priest in hearthstone or some color combination combining involving white or blue like UW Control Every once in a blue moon, you can find my playing League of Legends or any other game i haven't quite completed in my Steam library. Occasionally i'll take pictures of nature doing it's thing.
